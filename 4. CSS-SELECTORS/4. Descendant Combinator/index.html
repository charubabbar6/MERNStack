Featured Recipes

Paneer Butter Masala

A rich and creamy dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) simmered in a smooth tomato-based gravy enriched with butter and heavy cream. The gravy is flavored with spices like garam masala, coriander, and cumin, creating a comforting and indulgent dish.

Chana Masala

A hearty and flavorful curry made with chickpeas cooked in a spiced tomato and onion sauce. This dish is aromatic with spices such as cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala, and it often includes a touch of tamarind or lemon juice for added tang.

Dosa

A crispy, thin pancake made from fermented rice and lentil batter. It is traditionally served with a variety of chutneys and sambar (a spiced lentil soup). Dosas are a staple of South Indian cuisine and can be filled with various savory fillings, such as spiced potatoes.
