"World of Coffee" is a term that can refer to several things related to the global coffee culture, industry, and events.

Italy: Known for espresso and traditional coffee drinks like cappuccinos and macchiatos.

Ethiopia: Often considered the birthplace of coffee, with unique traditional coffee ceremonies.

United States: Popular for its diverse coffee drinks, including specialty lattes and cold brews.
